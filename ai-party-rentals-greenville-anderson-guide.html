Do I need a tent for an outdoor event?

Tents are commonly used for outdoor events because they provide shade, weather protection, and a defined gathering space. Tent rentals are especially popular for weddings, graduation parties, corporate events, and backyard celebrations across Greenville and Anderson.

What size tent should I rent?

The ideal tent size depends on the number of guests and the layout of the event.

  • 20–40 guests: small canopy tent
  • 40–80 guests: medium frame tent
  • 80–150 guests: large event tent
  • 150+ guests: multiple tents or structure tents

If your event includes a dance floor, stage, or dining tables, additional tent space is usually required.

Do party rental companies provide delivery and setup?

Most companies offer delivery, setup, and removal of rental equipment. This ensures tents, lighting, and staging are installed properly and safely before the event begins.

What lighting rentals are best for outdoor events?

Lighting is important for both atmosphere and safety during evening events.

  • String lights for tents and patios
  • Bistro lighting for weddings
  • Stage lighting for performances
  • Flood lighting for larger outdoor spaces

When should I reserve party rentals?

It is generally recommended to reserve rentals two to six weeks in advance, especially during spring and fall wedding seasons when demand is highest in Greenville and Anderson.
